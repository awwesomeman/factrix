title: Examples

Executable notebook recipes demonstrating one factrix research scenario each.
Notebook files in
[`examples/`](https://github.com/awwesomeman/factrix/tree/main/examples)
are the source of truth; the MkDocs pages in this section are rendered from
those notebooks during the docs build.

## Available recipes

- [Multi-factor screening](multi_factor_screening.md) - You have several candidate factors and want type-I error control under multiple testing.
- [Stock factor evaluation](stock_factor_evaluation.md) - Preprocess, neutralize, and evaluate a per-stock factor before strategy construction.
