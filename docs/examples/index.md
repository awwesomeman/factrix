Hand-edited recipes demonstrating one factrix research scenario each.
Each page on this site is the canonical write-up; the source `.ipynb`
files in [`examples/`](https://github.com/awwesomeman/factrix/tree/main/examples)
are runnable mirrors for those who want to step through interactively.

## Available recipes

- [Multi-factor screening](multi_factor_screening.md) - You have several candidate factors and want type-I error control under multiple testing.
- [Stock factor evaluation](stock_factor_evaluation.md) - Preprocess, neutralize, and evaluate a per-stock factor before portfolio construction.
